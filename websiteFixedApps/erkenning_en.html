What is the Erkenning App?

The Erkenning App is a representation of the RDW open data on accreditation data. Please note that the data may lag behind reality or may be incorrect. If there are any complaints, please contact the source (RDW).

How can the app be used?

This app can be used to search all companies with RDW accreditations within one location or filtered by one specific accreditation. For example, you can check if the garage where you want to get your car MOT tested can really do this themselves or if they will outsource it to a colleague.

Perhaps you want something very specific, such as installing a gas system, getting an HGV MOT test, or even visiting an RDW-accredited photographer.
